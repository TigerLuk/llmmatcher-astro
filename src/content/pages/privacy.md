LLM Matcher ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website llmmatcher.com.

## Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Data

When you visit LLM Matcher, we automatically collect certain information through **Google Analytics 4**, including:

- Pages visited and time spent on each page
- Your general geographic region (country/city level)
- Device type, browser, and operating system
- Referring website
- Interactions with site features (GPU selected, affiliate links clicked)

This data is collected via cookies and is used solely to understand how visitors use the site so we can improve it.

### Information You Provide

- **Email address**: If you subscribe to our newsletter, we store your email address in Cloudflare KV to send you updates. You can unsubscribe at any time.
- **Votes**: If you vote on the community leaderboard, we record your vote along with a rate-limiting identifier (IP-based, hashed) in Cloudflare D1. No personally identifiable information is stored.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We share data only with:
